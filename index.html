What is IIC?

The Instution's Innovation Council (IIC) plays a crucial role in implementing the vision of the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MoE-IC) at the institutional level. Our council is dedicated to:

Driving Institutional Innovation
Develop and promote strategies that foster a culture of creativity and innovation within our institution.
Support Research and Development
Encourage and facilitate research activities and technological advancements that align with the industry needs and societal challenges.
Engaging Stakeholders
Collaborate with faculty, students, and external partners to drive innovative projects and solutions.
Monitoring Progress
Access and review the impact of innovation initiatives to ensure continuous improvement and alignment with broader goals.
